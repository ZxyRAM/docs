Welcome to Google. Once your account is ready, a handful of quick actions will set you up for everything else — from organizing research to collaborating with your team. Follow the steps below to get your workspace running, run your first search, and bring a colleague on board.

<Tip>
  Speed up every step with keyboard shortcuts. Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> (or <kbd>⌘</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> on macOS) to open the universal command palette from anywhere in the app. From there you can jump to any page, create items, or trigger actions — no mouse required.
</Tip>

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first workspace">
    A workspace is your private (or shared) environment for organizing searches, collections, and teammates.

    1. Sign in to [google.com](https://google.com) and click **New Workspace** in the left sidebar.
    2. Enter a **Workspace name** — for example, your team or project name.
    3. Choose a visibility setting: **Private** (only invited members) or **Shared** (anyone in your organization).
    4. Click **Create Workspace**.

    Your new workspace opens automatically. You will see an empty dashboard ready for your first search.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run your first search">
    With your workspace open, use the search bar at the top of the page to query any topic, URL, or keyword.

    1. Click the **Search** bar (or press <kbd>/</kbd> to focus it instantly).
    2. Type a query — for example, `quarterly report 2024` — and press <kbd>Enter</kbd>.
    3. Browse the results. Use the **Filters** panel on the left to narrow by date, source type, or tags.

    Google returns ranked results from across all connected sources. Hover any result card to preview the full content without leaving the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save a result to a collection">
    Collections let you bookmark and organize results so you can return to them later or share them with teammates.

    1. Hover over any result card in your search results.
    2. Click the **Save** icon (bookmark) that appears on the card.
    3. In the pop-up, select an existing collection or click **+ New Collection**, enter a name, and press <kbd>Enter</kbd>.
    4. The result is now pinned to that collection. Access all your collections from the **Collections** tab in the left sidebar.

    You can add notes to any saved result by opening the collection, clicking the item, and typing in the **Notes** field on the right panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite a teammate">
    Collaboration starts with getting your team into the same workspace.

    1. Open your workspace and click **Settings** (gear icon) in the top-right corner.
    2. Navigate to the **Members** tab.
    3. Click **Invite Members**, then enter one or more email addresses separated by commas.
    4. Choose a role — **Editor** or **Viewer** — for each invitee and click **Send Invitations**.

    Invitees receive an email with a direct link to join your workspace. Until they accept, they appear in the **Pending Invitations** list where you can resend or revoke the invitation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next?

Now that your workspace is set up, explore these areas to get even more from Google:

- **Integrations** — connect Slack, Zapier, and custom webhooks to automate your workflows.
- **Customization** — switch themes, create custom search filters, and configure a custom domain.
- **Collaboration** — assign roles, share collections with external users, and review the activity log.
