## What you can do with Google

Google's platform gives you a powerful set of tools to find and work with information:

- **Search** — Query across vast datasets and get structured, ranked results in milliseconds.
- **Organize** — Save, label, and group resources so your team always finds what they need.
- **Integrate** — Connect Google to your own applications using the REST API or webhooks.
- **Collaborate** — Share access with teammates, set permissions, and work together in real time.
- **Customize** — Tailor notifications, display settings, and preferences to match your workflow.
